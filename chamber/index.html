The Boksburg Chamber of Commerce


The Boksburg Chamber of Commerce (BK) is a non-profit organisation serving businesses and the community in the Boksburg area which forms part of the Ekurhuleni Metropolitan Municipality (EMM).

Our aim is to promote local socio-economic growth and development. To achieve this we are constantly looking for ways to create opportunities for businesses and the community to grow and prosper. We are proud of our track record of supporting entrepreneurs, local businesses and the community at large.
